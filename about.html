About OrthoCare India

Regional Leader in Orthopedic and Rehabilitation Solutions

Our Journey

OrthoCare India was established in 2008 by a team of healthcare professionals and engineers in Mumbai. What started as a small distribution center has evolved into a well-respected manufacturer and distributor of orthopedic products across Western and Central India.

Over the past 16 years, we have built strong relationships with over 200+ hospitals, clinics, and physiotherapy centers. We serve both institutional clients and individual patients, maintaining high standards of quality and customer service.

Our dedicated team includes orthopedic consultants, product specialists, quality control experts, and logistics professionals committed to delivering reliable orthopedic solutions to our customers.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, reliable orthopedic and rehabilitation products that help individuals recover and improve their mobility, supported by excellent customer service and technical guidance.

Our Vision

To be the most reliable orthopedic solutions provider in India, known for quality products, competitive pricing, and dependable customer support.

Our Core Values

Quality & Safety | Customer Focus | Reliability | Fair Pricing | Professional Support | Continuous Learning
