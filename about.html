About Modern Medical Aids

Manufacturing and exporting quality orthopedic appliances, rehabilitation support products, and hospital supplies from Delhi.

Our Story

Driven by quality, service, and rehabilitation expertise

Modern Medical Aids is a trusted Delhi-based manufacturer and exporter of orthopedic appliances, rehabilitation supports, and hospital supplies. Our company is dedicated to delivering products that help patients recover safely and comfortably.

We design and produce a wide range of products including cervical collars, back supports, fracture aids, neoprene supports, mobility devices, and physiotherapy accessories. Every item is manufactured with focus on durability, finish, and consistent performance.

Serving dealers, distributors, hospitals, and clinics across India and overseas, we are committed to responsive service, on-time delivery, and customer satisfaction with every order.
