Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes! We ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. Contact us for specific details about your destination.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all products. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact our support team to initiate a return.

Are your products medical-grade?

Yes, all our orthopedic products are medical-grade and FDA approved. They meet international healthcare standards and certifications.

How can I get expert advice on product selection?

Our healthcare professionals are available for consultation via phone, email, or online chat. They can recommend the best products for your needs.

Do you offer wholesale or bulk discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders, hospitals, clinics, and healthcare institutions. Please contact our sales team for more information.

How long does shipping usually take?

Standard delivery within the US typically takes 5-7 business days. Express delivery options are also available for urgent orders.
